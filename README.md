# personal_vlog

This is my personal space on GitHub where I share the projects 
I'm working on and the skills I'm building along the way. Some 
of them are open source, some are not (That's why I don't post EVERYTHING I do, but I do share some things with you ), but the ones you'll find 
here are the ones I'm most excited about.

The idea is simple: show what I'm learning, what I'm exploring, 
and what challenges I'm taking on. You'll find things related to 
computational methods, machine learning, data analysis, and 
whatever catches my attention next.

This repository is always under construction. New projects, new 
ideas, new things to figure out. That's kind of the point.

As of April 22, 2026, here is what I can share openly:

**MIT xPro — Architecture of Complex Systems** is a program where 
I explored decision making in high tech and engineering environments. 
The projects here reflect how I think about complex problems and 
the frameworks I use to approach them.

**Computational Methods 2** collects the solutions my team and I

**Portfolio** is where I started teaching myself HTML and Javascript 
just for fun. It became my personal corner of the internet where I 
show who I am, what I have built, and what I am currently working 
towards. Still evolving, always will be.
